Daddy Does Hair is more than just hairstyling—it’s a heartwarming celebration of father-daughter love. What started as Mike Worthington simply bonding with his daughter Asia through creative hairstyles has grown into a viral sensation, earning over 10 million views and features in top outlets like Cosmopolitan, HuffPost, and Allure. From pink braids to princess buns, Mike uses every style as a moment to build Asia’s confidence and imagination. Daddy Does Hair breaks stereotypes and shows the world that dads can be kings when it comes to their daughters’ crowns.
