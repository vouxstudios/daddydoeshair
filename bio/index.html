Mike Worthington is a proud father and the creator of Daddy Does Hair™, a platform that celebrates the powerful bond between fathers and daughters through the art of hairstyling. His journey began with a simple yet meaningful act: styling his daughter Asia Worthington’s hair as part of their daily routine. What started as a way to spend quality time quickly grew into a shared passion and a source of inspiration for millions.

Asia, full of personality and creativity, loves the unique and imaginative hairstyles her father creates. She has a vibrant love for the color pink and dreams of becoming a supermodel. For Mike, styling his daughter’s hair has always been more than just grooming — it’s a cherished opportunity to connect, build trust, and nurture her confidence.

Four years ago, Mike posted his first hair tutorial on social media. With no expectation of the impact it would have, the video quickly went viral, resonating with parents, caregivers, and educators around the world. Since that moment, he has consistently shared weekly content that showcases not only his hairstyling skills but also the joy and love between a father and his daughter.

To date, Mike has amassed over 10 million views across various social media platforms, with his work featured by major media outlets including UK Daily, Huffington Post, Allure, Cosmopolitan, and more.

Daddy Does Hair™ challenges outdated gender roles and erases the bias around fathers participating in their daughters’ care. It stands as a movement that empowers dads to be hands-on, loving, and actively involved. Through his work, Mike is helping to instill self-esteem in young girls from an early age — proving that the bond between a father and daughter can be built strand by strand, one style at a time.
